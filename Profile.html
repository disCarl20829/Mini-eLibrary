Our Story

Founded in 2022 by a group of book lovers in Tagum City, eLibrary started as a student project with a simple aim:
make curated reading material accessible to our community.
We began with a small selection of titles and a handful of volunteers helping to catalog books.
Over time the project grew into a local hub where students, teachers, and curious readers can discover both classic and contemporary works.

Vision

To inspire lifelong learning by providing an easy-to-use, community-focused digital library that connects readers to thoughtfully selected books and trustworthy information.

Mission
